Azpazeta

What is Azpazeta?

Azpazeta is the strategy-economic game where you can play thousands of adventures with the custom maps. Download the PDF manual (Spanish)

What comes with Azpazeta?

Azpazeta 2.0 comes with the complete map runner with support for OpenGL ES 2.0 graphics card. Also provides access to Azpazeta Market, a place where you can download maps.
